ABOUT THE SOCCER PREDICTOR
==========================

The Soccer Predictor is a smart football analytics platform that uses data
science and machine learning to explore match outcomes in an engaging and
accessible way.

By analysing historical results, upcoming fixtures, and statistical patterns,
the platform generates easy-to-understand predictions that help users explore
how data can be applied to the beautiful game. Whether you are a football
enthusiast, a data science learner, or simply curious about match trends, the
Soccer Predictor offers a fresh perspective on match analysis.

Designed as an educational and research-driven project, the Soccer Predictor
demonstrates how modern analytics and machine learning techniques can uncover
insights from football data. The platform emphasises transparency, responsible
use, and continuous improvement through data.

The Soccer Predictor is not a betting or gambling service. It does not provide
odds, accept wagers, or guarantee results. All predictions are informational
and experimental, created to support learning and exploration rather than
betting decisions.

Football meets data, insight, and innovation — all in one place.
